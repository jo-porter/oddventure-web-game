In it you find three doors and a spinning wheel divided into thirds. Next to it, a message reads:

"Life is a game of chance, the throw of a dice from another's hand. To believe yourself master of your own universe is madness itself. Yet still we persist, and so shall you....Spin the wheel my child.""
