She holds the bottle out and lets go. You catch it, just. Prying the cork out of the bottle neck a bitter smell wafts up to you. You glance up to the child, hoping her expression will give something away...but she's gone.

With nothing else left to do, you take a deep breath and down the strange substance. Your body begins to tingle and you fear you may have made a terrible mistake, but then you notice that your body has taken on the same translucence as the child's. You approach one of the doors and reach out to it. As you do, your hand passes straight through. With your new found powers, which way will you go?
